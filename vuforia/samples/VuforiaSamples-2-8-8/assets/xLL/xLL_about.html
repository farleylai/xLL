xLL: Flashcards reminds users of reviewing learned vocabulary in spaced repetition.

Key Functionality

  • Show flashcards based on learning progress and review history.
  • Flip between word/phrase or definitions as configured.

Targets

Word List

Instructions

  • Click flip to show the other side.
  • Click next to review the next flashcard.
