xLL
===

eXtreme Language Learning (xLL) is a language learning toolkit addressing the integration of
 - Listening
 - Speaking
 - and Vocabulary.

Users are free to learn from preferred media recordings.
A media player plays timed subtitles with appropriate vocabulary highlights and popup definitions.
A Speaking pattern matching with real speech evaluates your speaking capability.
A flashcard serves as your knowledge base and track your learning progress.
